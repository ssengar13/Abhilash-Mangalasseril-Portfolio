About

Innovating across industries, V Line Group is a dynamic conglomerate committed to delivering excellence in every endeavor.

Abhilash K. Nair is a visionary entrepreneur who has built a legacy of growth, innovation, and trust through the creation and leadership of the V Line Group of Companies. Abhilash Mangalasseril – Founder and Visionary Leader of V Line Group

The V Line Group: A Symbol of Trust and Innovation

Founded under the leadership of Abhilash, the V Line Group is a conglomerate of innovative businesses spanning multiple sectors, including engineering, travel, fuel, wealth management, FMCG, and media. Each venture within the V Line umbrella stands as a reflection of the company’s core values—reliability, expertise, and a dedication to superior quality.

Abhilash K. Nair’s entrepreneurial journey is rooted in his passion for innovation and his unwavering commitment to delivering value. From a young age, he believed in creating opportunities rather than waiting for them. His visionary leadership and hands-on approach have allowed him to build an empire that prioritizes customer trust, high-quality service, and social responsibility. With each new venture, Abhilash aims to leave a lasting impact, not only in business but also by inspiring others to follow their entrepreneurial dreams.

Today, Abhilash continues to expand his legacy through strategic growth and a focus on social responsibility. He believes that true success lies in uplifting the community and fostering an environment where people and businesses can thrive together, marking his leadership as not only business-oriented but also deeply community-focused.

Our Companies

Diverse Ventures, Unified Vision

  • V Line Builders and Engineers

    Leading the way in civil engineering, V Line Builders provides complete construction solutions for residential, commercial, and infrastructure projects, ensuring high standards and precision.

  • V Line Tours and Travels

    A premier travel agency offering personalized travel solutions for both domestic and international travelers. From customized packages to seamless logistics, V Line ensures memorable journeys.

  • V Line Fuels

    A trusted name in the fuel industry, committed to delivering high-quality petroleum products to businesses and individuals, ensuring efficient and timely service.

  • V Line Wealth Builders

    As an AMFI-registered mutual fund distributor, V Line Wealth Builders offers systematic investment plans (SIPs), SWPs, and insurance services, helping clients secure their financial future.

  • V Line Associates

    A growing network of marketing and canvassing executives that bridge the gap between investors and businesses, creating the right financial opportunities for mutual success.

  • V Line Foods and Marketing

    Serving the ever-evolving needs of the FMCG sector, V Line Foods is dedicated to providing quality products, backed by innovative marketing strategies.

  • V Line Media

    A creative hub specializing in advertising, digital marketing, branding, and media production, helping businesses enhance their visibility and engagement in a competitive market.
